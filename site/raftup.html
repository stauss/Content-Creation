RaftUp

Safety, Adventure, Community

Design is visual. Adventures are visceral. I think an app for sailing, socializing, and staying safe on the lake ought to work just as well outdoors as it does on your desk. For RaftUp I delivered just that. Anchored by their earnest desire to connect people on the water, we quickly turned their idea into a viable product. The result is nothing short of a social treasure map - one that takes users away from home, but never far from friends.
